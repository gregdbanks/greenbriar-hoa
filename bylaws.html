What Owners Can Do

CC&R Section 2.10, Prohibition of Employment or Other Pecuniary Gain "No part of the assessments or net earnings of the Association shall inure to the benefit of any Lot Owner or individual, except to the extent that Lot Owners receive the benefits from the maintenance, repair, operations, additions, alterations and improvement responsibility of the Association. No Lot Owner or any business in which a Lot Owner has an interest may receive a credit or compensation, directly or indirectly, for services rendered as an officer, director or employee of the Association."

Your Shield

Why serving on the board is safe. Articles VII and IX.

🛡

Indemnification

Art. VII-1: The Association indemnifies every Director, Officer, and Managing Agent against all loss, costs, and expenses, except gross negligence or willful misconduct.

📜

No Personal Liability on Contracts

Art. VII-2: Contracts made by the Board are made as agents for the members. Directors have no personal responsibility on any contract or commitment.

🎯

Insurance Coverage

Art. IV-3d: The Board shall obtain comprehensive liability insurance covering the entire premises, $100K per person, $300K per accident, $50K property damages.

You're protected. Do the right thing and the bylaws have your back.
